Indian Penal Code, 1860 – Key Sections

Section 498A – Husband or relative of husband of a woman living with her, subjecting her to cruelty: “Whoever, being the husband or relative of husband of a woman, subjects such woman to cruelty shall be punished…” Cruelty includes harassment or coercion for dowry or willful conduct to drive her to suicide.

Section 375 – Rape: “A man is said to commit ‘rape’ if he has sexual intercourse with a woman under circumstances such as...” (list of seven clauses).

Section 376 – Punishment for rape: Specifies minimum and maximum imprisonment for rape.

Section 377 – Unnatural offences: Punishment for voluntary carnal intercourse against the order of nature.

Section 503 – Criminal intimidation: Intent to cause alarm, threat of injury to person, reputation, or property.

Section 506 – Punishment for criminal intimidation.
