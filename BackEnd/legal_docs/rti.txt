Right to Information Act, 2005 – Core Provisions

Section 3 – Right to information accessible from authorities.

Section 4 – Obligations of public authorities for manual, electronic disclosure, suo motu publication.

Section 6 – Request for obtaining information; timelines (30 days, 48 hours for life threats).

Section 7 – Disposal of request; grounds for refusal (life, privacy, commercial).

Section 8 – Exempted categories (national security, personal privacy).

Section 19 – Appeal and review mechanisms; & timelines.

Section 20 – Penalties: fine of Rs 250/day for delay.
