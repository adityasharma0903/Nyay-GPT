Muslim Women (Protection of Rights on Marriage) Act, 2019 – Key Points

Instant Triple Talaq banned: pronouncement of “talaq-talaq-talaq” any form is invalid.

Section 3 – Decree of talaq void.

Section 4 – Offence punishable with imprisonment up to 2 years and fine.

Section 5 – Subsistence allowance to wife and dependent children.

Section 6 – Custody of minor children.

Section 7 – No abatement in criminal proceedings even after reconciliation.

Case law: Shayara Bano vs Union of India (2017) – SC judgments on triple talaq.
